What is TP?

click the underlined words for it's definition

TP is an app that helps you to study and make your day a nicer one.It includes :

A Pomodoro timer Which is used to study for 30 mins and take a break for 5 mins with a Countdown timer

A clock 12 houred format

A Notepad For noting information and writing your personal notes

A Time-table maker To create your timetables

A To-do list a list of things that one wants to get done or to do

So, study well! all the best!
